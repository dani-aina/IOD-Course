Welcome to Bookish!

Bookish is a web application that allows users to search for books, view details about them, and manage their personal book collection. Whether you're an avid reader or just looking for your next great read, Bookish has you covered.

To get started, simply use the search bar to find books by title, author, or ISBN. You can also create an account to save your favorite books and keep track of your reading progress.
